Our Mission

To advance interdisciplinary research on the neural basis of decision making. We hope to understand how economic behavior shapes our understanding of the brain and how neuroscientific discoveries might guide economic models.

Here, you can find information on upcoming workshops and courses related to Neuroeconomics at Stanford.

Converging Disciplines

  • Psychology

    Professor Brian Knutson is investigating the neural basis of emotion. He leads the Symbiotic Project on Affective Nueroscience (SPAN) Lab at Stanford University.

  • Economics

    Professor Antonio Rangel studies the computational and biological basis of decision making. He leads the Rangel Neureconomics Laboratory (RNL) at Caltech.

  • Neurobiology

    Professor Bill Newsome hopes to understand the neuronal processes that mediate visual perception and behavior. He leads the Newsome Lab at Stanford Medicine.
